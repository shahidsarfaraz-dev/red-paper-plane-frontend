Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have questions about our video brochures? We've compiled answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our video brochures.

How do I get the video file to you?

Video files are usually too large to transfer through traditional email or uploads. Once you’ve placed your order with us, a project manager will contact you to arrange the transfer of your video file via WeTransfer or other file-sharing services.

How many minutes of playtime?

The length of your video will depend on the resolution, frame rate, and bit rates. The available memory decides whether your video will fit on a particular memory chip size. This chart discusses video sizes in greater detail.

We want to do the mailing ourselves; how can we upload the videos?

Once you place your order with us, a project manager will contact you to arrange to transfer your video file via WeTransfer or other file-sharing services. After your video brochures are manufactured, we will ship them to the address you supplied when placing your order.

What is the difference between quick turn and value option video brochures?

Quick turn video brochures can be ready in as few as seven business days. The production process for our value option video brochures takes 4-5 weeks.

Why do value option video brochures take so long to produce?

Value option video brochures come in more designs and styles and take longer to produce than quick turn videos. They are a smart choice for companies looking for a quality product at an affordable price.

Can we order in bulk, and have you upload several different videos?

It is possible to include more than one video in your video brochure, but it depends on the chip's available memory size. We recommend contacting us using our custom quote or contact form for large or custom orders.
