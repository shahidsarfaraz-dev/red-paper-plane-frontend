Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have questions about our video brochures? We've compiled answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about our video brochures.

How do I get the video file to you?

Video files are usually too large to transfer through traditional email or uploads. Once you’ve placed your order with us, a project manager will contact you to arrange the transfer of your video file via WeTransfer or other file-sharing services.

How many minutes of playtime?

The length of your video will depend on the resolution, frame rate, and bit rates. The available memory decides whether your video will fit on a particular memory chip size. This chart discusses video sizes in greater detail.

We want to do the mailing ourselves; how can we upload the videos?

Here is how to upload your video:

  1. Plug the supplied USB cord into the video brochure.
  2. Plug the USB cord into your computer.
  3. The video brochure will launch as an external hard drive.
  4. Locate the test video on the video brochure and delete it.
  5. Be sure to empty your trash, especially on a Mac, to free up space.
  6. Drag your new video into the same location as the test video was.
  7. Eject the video brochure as you would an external hard drive.
  8. Play your video to be sure everything looks good.
What is the difference between quick turn and value options video brochures?

Quick Turnaround Video Brochures can be ready in as few as seven business days. The production process for our value option video brochures takes 4-5 weeks.

Why do value option video brochures take so long to produce?

You can also add enhancements like foil, embossing, debossing, spot treatment, insert pages, and pockets.

Are tariffs covered in your Value Option Video Brochures?

Our pricing is fully inclusive and transparent—what you see is what you pay. All tariffs are covered, with no hidden fees or surprise charges. We believe in upfront pricing, ensuring a hassle-free purchasing experience so you can focus on making a lasting impression with your marketing materials.

Can we order in bulk, and have you upload several different videos?

It is possible to include more than one video in your video brochure, but it depends on the chip's available memory size. We recommend contacting us using our custom quote or contact form for large or custom orders.
